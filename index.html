Breif History of Drawing

Drawing is the oldest form of human expression

“It is believed that drawing was used as a specialised form of communication before the invention of the written language, demonstrated by the production of cave and rock paintings around 30,000 years ago.” -Quote From Wikipedia

In early days, people used sharp props to paint on stones. As our world became more advanced, we started to use pen, pencils, color pencils, watercolor, oil painting, etc.

Motivation

The reason I choose to do visual arts is because I am interested in learning how art and technology can be combined together and what impact will come out if they are connected. Technology is playing an important role in visual arts. It makes an illustrator's life simpler by providing tech tools like drawing apps and it also supports beginners to become more professionals. In conclusion, technology is helpful to visual artists to find a career and visual art itself. It opens a new door for arts and it’s being introduced to more people in the world through technology.

Technologies In Art

App

Procreate

This is a drawing app that can be download from IOS, but you need to purchase $9.99 to download it from App Store. It's a great place where you can start your digital drawing!

App

SketchBook Pro

This is a free digital drawing software that you can download it on your computers, tablets and phone. Easy for beginners to create illustrations.

App

Adobe Photoshop

Super helpful for all painters especially graphic designers. It allows users to create designs as well as helps in designing logos, marketing materials for businesses.

Tech Tool

Tablets

Tablet has the perfect screen for drawing digitally when it comes to size.

Tech Tool

Digital Pencils

Digital pencils allow painters to draw smoothly on their tablets.
